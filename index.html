About Us

Tibatrade, established in 2017, stands at the forefront of food supply and distribution, with a steadfast commitment to excellence. With three thriving branches, our company has quickly become a trusted name in the industry, renowned for its quality products and efficient services. Our dedication to customer satisfaction extends beyond just supplying goods; it's about building lasting relationships. With a team of passionate professionals, we go above and beyond to understand and fulfill the unique requirements of each client, striving to exceed expectations at every turn.

Our Mission

At Tibatrade, our mission is to redefine the standards of excellence in food supply and distribution. We are committed to sourcing the finest quality products, delivering efficient services, and building lasting relationships with our customers.

Our Vision

Our vision at Tibatrade is to become the premier provider of food supply and distribution solutions, setting the benchmark for quality, reliability, and innovation in the industry. We aspire to expand our reach globally .
